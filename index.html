About Us

Importation & Installation of Finishing Materials

We are a Mauritius-based company specialising in the importation and installation of premium finishing materials. We work with homeowners who have just completed construction and need everything installed — from wiring and plumbing through to kitchen fitouts, bathroom fixtures, appliances and final touches.

Our mission is simple: take the stress out of home completion. We import quality materials at competitive prices, coordinate every trade, and hand over a home that is 100% move-in ready. Johan and Kevin personally oversee each project to ensure nothing is missed.
